================================================================================
Matrix-SHT DNA Analysis — Suggested Reading Order
================================================================================

These documents represent working notes and observations from an ongoing
anaylsis of DNA geometry and mechanics. They are not intended as a finished
manuscipt, but as material for scientific feedback and discussion.

Suggested reading order:

1. Executive_Summary.txt
   A high-level overview of the observed biological signals and motivation.

2. Scientific_Status_Report.md
   The main report describing methodolgy, population-scale observations,
   and biological interpretations.

3. discovery_results.txt
   Quantative observations related to mechanical coherence and signal
   propagation along DNA.

4. entropy_results.txt
   Analysis of spectral entropy varience and its correlation with functional
   versus structural DNA states.

5. snap_results.txt
   Rare or extreme configuations ("spectral snaps") identified as large
   deviations from canonical DNA baselines.

6. Dataset_Information.txt
   Description of the PDB-derived dataset, scope, and processing statistics.

7. PDB_Series_Audit.txt
   Audit log of recent PDB structures and their spectral classification.

8. Technical_Definitions.md
   Reference definitions for paramters, units, and terminology used
   throughout the analysis.

All feedback—especially critical feedback on biological interpretation—is
very welcome.
================================================================================
